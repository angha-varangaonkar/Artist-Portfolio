About Myself

Hi, I’m Milind Varangaonkar, a realistic artist from Udaipur, Rajasthan. My passion for art started at the JJ School of Art in Mumbai, where I learned to appreciate the beauty in the human form and nature’s landscapes.

After my studies, I worked as an illustrator at Creator Advertising, but I realized I wanted to express myself in a more personal way. That’s when I turned to realistic art, which truly speaks to me.

In my paintings, I try to capture the true essence of whatever I’m working on—whether it’s the details of a face or the majesty of a natural scene.
